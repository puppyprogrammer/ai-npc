# Drop your avatar here

Place a VRM model at `apps/demo/public/avatar.vrm` (the demo loads `/avatar.vrm`).

Any VRM works — e.g. export one from [VRoid Studio](https://vroid.com/en/studio) (free), or grab a
sample from the [@pixiv/three-vrm samples](https://github.com/pixiv/three-vrm). The model needs the
standard VRM vowel expressions (`aa/ih/ou/ee/oh`) + `blink` for lip-sync (VRoid exports include them).
